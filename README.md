# Email-Spam-Detection-
Email has become one of the most essential communication tools in both personal and professional life.
Every day, millions of emails are exchanged across the world for information sharing, marketing, business transactions,
and social interaction. However, with the increase in digital communication, the problem of spam emails—unsolicited, irrelevant, 
or fraudulent messages—has also grown significantly.
These spam emails not only clutter users’ inboxes but also pose serious security risks such as phishing attacks, malware distribution, and data theft.
